10 Stage Purification Process

How we ensure the highest standards of purity in every drop of riveric™

01

Careful Collection

Collected water undergoes quality checks at source to ensure optimal raw water standards.

02

Ozonisation

Ozone kills the harmful bacteria and viruses through advanced oxidation processes.

03

Sand Filtration

Removes unwanted particles and suspended solids up to 30 microns for initial clarity.

04

Carbon Filtration

Gets rid of colour, odour and harmful pesticides through double-stage carbon absorption.

05

Reverse Osmosis

Excess minerals and dissolved salts are removed to achieve the perfect TDS balance.

06

Mineralisation

We add back the right amount of vital minerals (Magnesium & Potassium) for health and taste.

07

Micron-filtration

Ensures that even the smallest micron-particles (upto 0.45 microns) are removed.

08

Re-ozonisation

Helps to maintain purity and quality in sealed condition of the bottle for long-term safety.

09

Careful Bottling

Bottles are blown on site and packaged using highly modernized equipment to avoid contamination.

10

Quality Control

Every bottle has a code for batch tracking. Stringent quality checks are done for every batch.
